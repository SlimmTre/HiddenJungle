About Us

At Hidden Jungle, based in Killeen Texas, we seek the creative, raw, and untamed potential that emanates in all clients. We are compiled with some of the most talented engineers, local producers, singer songwriters and videographer’s. Hidden Jungle is more than just a company. We are more than just a label. We are a multimedia platform invested in finding your inner BEAST, and promoting your growth. Hidden Jungle is here to make sure your hidden talents are heard! Our services include Quality Mixing, Mastering, Recording, Editing, Producing, and Promoting.

Whether you’re an artist trying to record a new single, to an independent artist trying to promote a collaborative album we’ve got you covered. Videos, mixes, and a place for everyone to thrive, all available here. Be sure to check out all of our offers and services. BE SEEN NOT HIDDEN!
